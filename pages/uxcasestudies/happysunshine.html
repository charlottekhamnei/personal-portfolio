Happy Sunshine: A Mobile App Design

Happy Sunshine is an mobile app prototype for a food truck local to Drexel campus.

Project Team

The project team members included Nikki Sandeep, Lianna Wang, Preeti Shenoy, and Charlotte Khamnei. We all had similar roles in terms of research, design, and prototyping.

Preliminary Research

Each team member did extensive individual research, and we compiled our findings. Notable research included a Fly-on-the-Wall excercise, customer interviews, a business model, moodboards, and sketches.
