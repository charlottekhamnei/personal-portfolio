Back on my Feet: Co-Op Apr - Sep 2021

During my Co-Op with Back on my Feet, I was responsible for creating a variety of different types of graphics and digital content. As a part of the communications team, my role focused on social media, volunteer recruitment, rebranding, event materials, and race-day preparation. For more information about BomF, visit their website here.

Social Media

When creating social media content for BomF, it was important to consider the audience. The social media accounts were geared towards runners participating in BomF events. Here is a collection of social media tiles I created for race participants to post to show their participation and support of BomF.

Recruitment

A large part of BomF's operations depend on volunteers and their members. Recruitment must be handled with integrity, approachability, and passion. My designs incorperated rebranding I created, a whole lot of vital information, and eye-catching elements.

Rebrand

An exciting oppertunity was given to me to pitch a brand redesign for Back on my Feet. I considered the athletic/running element of the brand and their dedication to giving back.

FundRacing Toolkit

During my time at BomF, an important project was working on an interactive toolkit to FundRacers/race participants for their social media outreach and personal promotion.

Event Materials

These designs include sponserships decks for private events.

Race Day Materials

A few of the many race-day graphics needed during my time at BomF include a tank top design for multiple races across the country and a teeshirt, race bib, and thank you card for Run Fest.
