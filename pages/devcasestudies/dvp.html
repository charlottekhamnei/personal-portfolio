About
Dragon Virtual Pet: A Mobile Wellness Game
Dragon Virtual Pet was a devleopment project aiming to encourage the healthy habits of users by having them track their habits. By imputting data into the app they can grow a different dragon from an egg based on the healthy habit type.
Our Team

Team members included: Charlotte Khamnei (Designer/Developer), Dheekshu Senthamilselvan, Jermey Ashley (Developer), and Glory Foster (Developer). My Role in this project ranged from creating graphics, coding essential functionality, and applying final details like music produced by Chris __.

Process/Tools

Our team used Kivy for Python, and Python as our main developement tools. We also recorded all of our work and managed our Agile project on GitLab (Drexel's GitHub). Here is a link to the repository.

Design Process
User Stories

Following the Agile method, our team generated user stories as a way to focus and organize our tasks. Each user story is comprised of a description, size, and acceptance criteria.

Activity Report

Documentation was a vital part of this project. It was crucial to document our code with comments and document what work was completed in our activity reports.

Team Metrics

Our team measured our progress by using a burndown chart for each sprint and a work-life balance metric.

MVP Prototype

Our team was proud to present our final MVP. Complete with music, advanced functionality, and a user guide or app demonstrated all the goals we set.
